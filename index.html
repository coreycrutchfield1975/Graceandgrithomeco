About Michelle

Your Neighbor,
Your Handywoman

Hi, I'm Michelle Crutchfield — owner of Grace & Grit Home Co. right here in Poplar Bluff, MO. Born and raised in this community, I built this business on hard work, honesty, and a whole lot of faith.

Horses, cattle, wide-open land — that's the life I love. And whether I'm running a tractor across a field or brushing fresh paint on your walls, I bring that same ranch-work ethic to every single job.

Grace & Grit means doing right by people. You get Michelle — not a crew, not a middleman. Just honest service with my name behind it.
