About Michelle

Your Neighbor,
Your Handywoman

Hi, I'm Michelle Crutchfield — owner of Grace & Grit Home Co. right here in Poplar Bluff, MO. I started this business because I believe every home deserves care, and every customer deserves honest, hard-working service you can count on.

From painting a bedroom to bushhog-clearing a field, I bring the same dedication and faith-driven work ethic to every single job. I run the tractor, I swing the brush, and I stand behind every project with my name on it.

Whether it's your home or your land — I'm here to help it shine.
