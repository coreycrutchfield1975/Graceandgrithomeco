Seasonal Guide for Your Property

Prepared for: The Johnson Family Property: 123 Elm St, Poplar Bluff, MO Year:
🌸 Spring
  • Inspect roof for winter damage, missing shingles
  • Clean gutters and downspouts
  • Check exterior caulking around windows & doors
  • Inspect deck or porch for rot, loose boards, or nails
  • Test and reset GFCI outlets
  • Service HVAC / replace air filter
  • Check sump pump is operational
  • Inspect and clean dryer vent
  • Test smoke and CO detectors
  • Flush hot water heater sediment
  • Inspect foundation for cracks or settling
  • Power wash siding, driveway, or walkways
☀️ Summer
  • Check A/C unit — clean coils, ensure drainage
  • Inspect window and door screens for tears
  • Trim trees and shrubs away from house
  • Inspect and seal driveway cracks
  • Test outdoor outlets and lighting
  • Check sprinkler / irrigation system
  • Inspect attic for signs of moisture or pests
  • Grease garage door hinges and tracks
  • Check weatherstripping on all doors
  • Inspect crawl space vents
  • Clean refrigerator coils
  • Service lawn equipment
🍂 Fall
  • Inspect and clean chimney / fireplace
  • Service heating system before first use
  • Drain and store garden hoses; shut off exterior spigots
  • Clean gutters after leaves fall
  • Inspect roof again before winter
  • Caulk any gaps around windows, doors, pipes
  • Check attic insulation and ventilation
  • Replace furnace filter
  • Test smoke and CO detectors; replace batteries
  • Winterize sprinkler system
  • Seal gaps where pests could enter
  • Store patio furniture or cover for winter
❄️ Winter
  • Know where your main water shutoff is located
  • Insulate exposed pipes in unheated spaces
  • Keep thermostat at 55°F+ when away
  • Check roof after heavy snowfall for ice dams
  • Clear snow from HVAC exhaust / intake vents
  • Test garage door auto-reverse safety feature
  • Check fire extinguisher charge
  • Inspect for drafts around outlets on exterior walls
  • Run water in rarely-used fixtures to prevent freezing
  • Stock emergency supplies (flashlights, batteries)
  • Review homeowner's insurance policy
  • Schedule spring projects now — book early!
